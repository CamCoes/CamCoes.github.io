My 3D Printer

This is my 3D printer, it is a cr10s. Over the past 10 months I have been playing with it and modifying it. I have modified my printer to use a clone E3D V6 hotend, stepper dampeners, klipper firmware, and a bltouch. During the 2020 Coronovirus pandamic I lead design and communications of a group of people who were using their own personal printers to print faceshield. Our teams efforts ended when we produced our 10600th face shield due to a limit in money and supplies. On top of the faceshield production and design I have also 3d printed many of my own projects (you can read about it in my CAD section).

   

My history with First Robotics

I have been a part of the first robotics team 3539 for four years now. For the first three years I was a programmer on the programming subteam. While I was programming I would help with CAD, Electrical, and Fabrication. For my forth year I was lead programmer, my job was to lead a group of programmers in programming basic and advanced robot controls. While I continue my education I am planning on mentoring the robotics team.
